Akkúrat sells handpicked products from the most current and exciting designers at each time from Iceland and the Nordic countries along with few selected ones from the international design scene.
Akkúrat collaborates with designers and artists in creating a living space with happenings and exhibitions. A place to meet others and to be inspired.
The store will open in May 2017.
